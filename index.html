Tuesday, June 16
Sandos Free Day: Choose Your Own Adventure
FlexibleBeach recoveryOptional excursion
🗺️
Best default: stay at Sandos
After two big park days and a cenote, a true pool-and-beach day may be the smartest choice. This also lets Sandos feel like a destination instead of just a place to sleep.
Highest “wow” option: Rio Secreto
Great if you want a more polished underground river/cave experience and everyone still has adventure energy.
Easiest cenote add-on: Cenote Azul
Good if you want an open-air cenote without committing to another full structured tour.
Animal/nature choice: Akumal turtles
Potentially memorable, but more logistics and rules. Better if sea turtles are a true priority.
Light evening option: Playa del Carmen
Souvenirs, people-watching, and a change of scenery without making the whole day about an excursion.

My vote

  • Morning: beach/pool
  • Afternoon: more resort
  • Evening: optional 5th Avenue

Only book more if

  • Everyone slept well
  • Kids want one more adventure
  • You are not tired of water shoes
